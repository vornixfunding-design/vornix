// Our Story

ABOUT VORNIX

We are not just funding traders. We are building the infrastructure that turns trading into a legitimate profession — worldwide.

VORNIX was founded by Jatin Sharma after identifying a fundamental and universal gap in the global trading industry: millions of talented traders enter markets every year, yet the overwhelming majority fail — not because they lack skill or intelligence, but because the system around them is broken.

Trading is treated as gambling in most parts of the world. Yet risk exists in every profession. An engineer studies for years with no guarantee of employment. A doctor spends a decade in education. The difference is that those professions have structured pathways — and trading does not. Not yet.

VORNIX was created to change that. By combining rigorous evaluation, real capital access and a technology ecosystem, we are building the infrastructure that makes trading a legitimate, structured, scalable profession — for anyone, anywhere in the world.

"Trading is one of the fastest-growing financial activities on earth. Yet most participants fail — not because they lack talent, but because no structured system exists to develop them."

— JATIN SHARMA, FOUNDER · VORNIX
