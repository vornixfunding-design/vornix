Every program follows the same journey. Choose your evaluation, prove your edge, get funded and scale.

02
Single Evaluation

Hit 10% profit. Stay within 3% trailing daily loss and 8% static max drawdown. No time limit. No minimum days. News trading allowed.

03

// Universal Rules

GLOBAL TRADING RULES

These rules apply to every VORNIX account regardless of program.

All Instruments

Forex, Indices, Commodities (Gold, Oil, Silver), Crypto and Stocks — tradeable on every program.

Trailing Daily Loss

Calculated from the highest equity point of the trading day. Trails upward only. Resets each new trading day.

Static Max Drawdown

Calculated from the initial account balance only. Never adjusts regardless of profits generated.

Fast Withdrawals

Available as soon as you generate profit on a funded account. Processed via crypto, Wise or bank in 1–3 business days.

Scaling Program

Every 10% cumulative profit on a funded account = 25% automatic increase in allocation. Up to $100K maximum.

Evaluation Reset

If evaluation rules are breached, purchase a reset at a discounted fee and continue from the same phase immediately.
