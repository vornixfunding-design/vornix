TRADING NEEDS A STRUCTURED PATHWAY

Medicine has a degree. Engineering has a curriculum. Trading has YouTube videos and unverified instructors. VORNIX Academy changes that — permanently.

PROBLEM 01
❌
Unregulated Mentors

Anyone can sell a trading course. No track record required. No accountability. Most traders are taught by people who have never traded professionally.

PROBLEM 02

THE FIX

// The Mentor System

FUNDED TRADERS BECOME VETTED MENTORS

Every mentor must have a verified live trading track record on a VORNIX funded account before they can teach. No exceptions.

✓ Verified Track Record

Every mentor must demonstrate a minimum of 3 months of consistent, profitable trading on a VORNIX funded account before being approved to teach.

✓ Quality Controlled Content

All course content is reviewed before publishing. No unproven strategies. No get-rich-quick content. Every lesson must be backed by real trade data.

✓ Ongoing Performance Review

Mentors are reviewed quarterly. If funded account performance drops significantly, mentor status is suspended pending review. Real accountability enforced.

✓ Global Coverage

Content covers global markets — Forex, Indices, Commodities, Crypto — applicable to traders in any region or time zone worldwide.

✓ Multi-Language Support

The Academy is designed to serve traders globally. Content and support will be available in multiple languages to eliminate language as a barrier.

✓ Community Integration

Academy graduates get access to the VORNIX funded trader community — live strategy discussions, performance sharing and collective improvement.
