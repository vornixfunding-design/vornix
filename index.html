GLOBAL TRADING RULES

These rules apply to every VORNIX account regardless of plan.

All Instruments

Forex, Indices, Commodities (Gold, Oil, Silver), Crypto and Stocks — tradeable on every plan.

Trailing Daily Loss

Calculated from the highest equity point of the trading day. Trails upward only. Resets each new day.

Static Max Drawdown

Calculated from the initial account balance only. Never adjusts regardless of profits generated.

Profit Withdrawals

Available from Day 1 of a funded account. Processed via crypto, Wise or bank in 1–3 business days.

Scaling Program

Every 10% cumulative profit on a funded account = 25% automatic increase in allocation. Up to $100K.

Challenge Reset

// FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

Which challenge plan should I choose?+
Speed: 1-Step. Balance + news trading: 2-Step. Maximum structure with strict rules: 3-Step. Cost concern: Partial Payment at 35% upfront.
What is the 15% consistency rule exactly?+
On 3-Step and Partial plans, no single profitable trade may contribute more than 15% of total accumulated profit. If total profit = $1,000, no single trade can have made more than $150. Active in all phases and the funded account.
What is the news trading rule on 3-Step and Partial?+
No trades may be opened within 5 minutes before or after a high-impact news event. If such a trade closes in profit — the profit is disqualified. If in loss — the full loss is counted. Applies in evaluation phases and the live funded account.
How does the trailing daily loss work?+
The daily loss limit is calculated from your highest equity point during that trading day. It trails upward as equity grows but never moves downward. It resets at the start of each new trading day.
How does the Partial Payment plan work?+
Pay 35% of the challenge fee upfront. Complete all 3 evaluation phases. Once funded and your first profit withdrawal is processed, the remaining 65% of the fee is automatically deducted from that withdrawal. No additional payment ever comes out of pocket.
When can I withdraw profits from a funded account?+
Profit withdrawals are available from Day 1 of your funded account. Processed via cryptocurrency, Wise and bank transfer within 1–3 business days.
Can traders from India join VORNIX?+
Absolutely. VORNIX accepts traders globally. Payouts are available via crypto and Wise for international traders. VORNIX was built with India's rapidly growing trading community specifically in mind.
When do AI tools and the education platform launch?+
The evaluation and funding platform is live now. All technology products are in active development. Join the waitlist via the contact form above to be among the first to access these products when they launch.
