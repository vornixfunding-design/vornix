// Trading Rules

VORNIX RULES

Every rule clearly explained. No hidden conditions. Read this before starting any challenge — know exactly what is and is not allowed on every plan.

// Section 01

TRADING RULES

These rules apply to every account — evaluation and funded — unless a specific plan exception is noted.

📉 Daily Loss Limit — Trailing

Calculated from the highest equity point of that trading day. Trails upward only. Resets at 00:00 server time each day.

🛑 Max Drawdown — Static

Calculated from the initial account balance only. Never adjusts regardless of profits made.

📊 Consistency Rule

1-Step & 2-Step: No single trade may contribute more than 40% of total profit.

3-Step & Partial: No single trade may contribute more than 15% of total profit.

📅 Minimum Trading Days

1-Step: No minimum days.
2-Step & 3-Step: Minimum 3 trading days per phase.
Partial: Minimum 5 trading days per phase.

🕐 No Time Limit

All VORNIX evaluation plans have no time limit. Take as long as you need to pass, provided you stay within drawdown rules.

📈 Tradeable Instruments

All plans allow: Forex, Indices, Commodities (Gold, Silver, Oil), Crypto and Stocks.

// Section 02

PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES

🚫 News Trading — 3-Step & Partial Only

Trades within 5 minutes before or after a high-impact news event: profit is disqualified, loss is fully counted. Allowed on 1-Step and 2-Step.

🚫 Account Sharing

Only the registered account holder may trade. Account sharing, copy trading from external signals, or third-party access is strictly prohibited.

🚫 Exploiting Platform Errors

Exploiting platform errors, incorrect pricing, latency arbitrage, or any technical vulnerability is prohibited. Such trades will be reversed and the account terminated.

🚫 Tick Scalping & HFT

Strategies holding trades for less than 2 minutes as a primary strategy are not permitted. This rule targets algorithmic exploitation only.

🚫 Opposite Account Hedging

Hedging between two VORNIX accounts simultaneously is prohibited. Hedging within a single account under normal trading strategy is allowed.

🚫 Weekend Gap Risk

Holding positions over the weekend exposes accounts to gap risk. Losses from weekend gaps are fully counted. Recommend closing before Friday 21:00 UTC.

// Section 03

PAYOUT & WITHDRAWAL RULES

💰 First Withdrawal — Day 1

Profit withdrawals available from Day 1 of your funded account. No minimum waiting period.

💰 Profit Split

1-Step: 95%.
2-Step: 90%.
3-Step & Partial: 80% rising to 90% after scaling milestones.

💰 Payment Methods

Payouts via: Cryptocurrency (USDT, USDC, BTC), Wise, and Bank Transfer. Processing: 1–3 business days after approval.

💰 Partial Payment Deduction

Remaining 65% of challenge fee automatically deducted from your first profit withdrawal only. All subsequent withdrawals receive the full split.

💰 Minimum Withdrawal

Minimum withdrawal amount is $50. No maximum withdrawal limit.

💰 KYC Required

Before first payout: government-issued photo ID and proof of address. One-time process only.

⚠ Risk Disclaimer

Trading financial instruments involves significant risk of loss and is not suitable for all investors. Past performance is not indicative of future results. Always trade responsibly.

On 3-Step and Partial plans, no single profitable trade may contribute more than 15% of total accumulated profit. If total profit = $1,000, no single trade can have made more than $150. Active in all phases and the funded account.

No trades may be opened within 5 minutes before or after a high-impact news event. If such a trade closes in profit — the profit is disqualified. If in loss — the full loss is counted.

The daily loss limit is calculated from your highest equity point during that trading day. It trails upward as equity grows but never moves downward. It resets at the start of each new trading day.
