// Trading Rules

VORNIX RULES

Every rule clearly explained. No hidden conditions. Read this before starting any evaluation — know exactly what is and is not permitted on every program.

// Section 01

CORE TRADING RULES

These rules apply to every account — evaluation and funded — unless a specific program exception is noted.

📉 Daily Loss Limit — Trailing

Calculated from the highest equity point of that trading day. If your equity reaches $10,500 during a day on a $10,000 account with a 5% daily loss limit — your floor becomes $9,975. Trails upward only. Resets at 00:00 server time each day.

🛑 Max Drawdown — Static

Calculated from the initial account balance only. Never adjusts regardless of profits generated. On a $10,000 account with 8% max drawdown, your absolute floor is always $9,200 — whether your account is at $10,000 or $12,000.

📊 Consistency Rule

1-Step & 2-Step: No single trade may contribute more than 40% of total accumulated profit.

3-Step & Partial: No single trade may contribute more than 15% of total accumulated profit.

📅 Minimum Trading Days

1-Step: No minimum days required.
2-Step & 3-Step: Minimum 3 trading days per phase.
Partial: Minimum 5 trading days per phase.
A trading day = at least one trade opened and closed.

🕐 No Time Limit

All VORNIX evaluation programs have no time limit. Take as long as you need to pass, provided you stay within the drawdown rules. There is no expiry date on any program.

📈 Tradeable Instruments

All programs allow: Forex (major, minor and exotic pairs), Indices (US30, NAS100, SPX500, UK100, GER40 etc), Commodities (Gold, Silver, Oil), Crypto (BTC, ETH, major pairs) and Stocks.

// Section 02

EA & ALGO RULES

EA and algorithmic trading rules vary by program. Read carefully.

✗ 1-Step — No EA/Algo

EA and algorithmic bots are not permitted on the 1-Step program. Manual trading only. Any trades executed via automated systems will be disqualified.

✗ 2-Step — No EA/Algo

EA and algorithmic bots are not permitted on the 2-Step program. Manual trading only. Any trades executed via automated systems will be disqualified.

✓ 3-Step — EA/Algo Allowed

EA and algorithmic trading is permitted on the 3-Step program in all phases and the funded account, provided all other rules (consistency, drawdown, no-news) are respected.

✓ Partial — EA/Algo Allowed

EA and algorithmic trading is permitted on the Partial Payment program in all phases and the funded account, provided all other rules (consistency, drawdown, no-news) are respected.

✗ Tick Scalping & HFT

Strategies that hold trades for less than 2 minutes as a primary method are not permitted on any program. This targets algorithmic exploitation only — normal short-term intraday trading is allowed.

⚠ Latency Arbitrage

Any strategy exploiting platform latency, price feed delays or broker errors is prohibited across all programs. Such trades will be reversed and the account terminated without refund.

// Section 03

PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES

These activities result in immediate account termination. There are no warnings.

🚫 News Trading — 3-Step & Partial Only

Trades opened or closed within 5 minutes before or after a high-impact news event: profit is disqualified. Loss is fully counted. News trading is fully allowed on 1-Step and 2-Step.

🚫 Account Sharing

Only the registered account holder may trade. Account sharing, copy trading from external signals, or third-party access is strictly prohibited and results in immediate termination.

🚫 Opposite Account Hedging

Hedging between two VORNIX accounts simultaneously is prohibited. Hedging within a single account under a normal trading strategy is permitted.

// Section 04

PAYOUT & WITHDRAWAL RULES

💰 Fast Withdrawals

Profit withdrawals are available from your funded account as soon as you generate profit. No arbitrary waiting periods. Processed via cryptocurrency, Wise and bank transfer within 1–3 business days.

💰 Profit Split

1-Step: 95% of all profits.
2-Step: 90% of all profits.
3-Step & Partial: 80% rising to 90% after scaling milestones.

💰 Payment Methods

Payouts via: Cryptocurrency (USDT, USDC, BTC — major networks), Wise (international bank transfers), and Bank Transfer. Processing time: 1–3 business days after approval.

💰 Partial Payment Deduction

If you used the Partial Payment program, the remaining 65% of the evaluation fee is automatically deducted from your first profit withdrawal only. All subsequent withdrawals receive the full split.

💰 Minimum Withdrawal

The minimum withdrawal amount is $50. There is no maximum withdrawal limit — you can withdraw your full profit balance at any time.

💰 KYC Verification

Before your first payout, complete KYC: government-issued photo ID and proof of address. One-time process only. All subsequent payouts process without re-verification.

⚠ Risk Disclaimer

Trading financial instruments involves significant risk of loss and is not suitable for all investors. Past performance is not indicative of future results. VORNIX evaluation accounts use simulated capital for the purpose of skill assessment. Funded accounts trade real capital subject to the profit split arrangement. Always trade responsibly.

We are not just funding traders. We are building the infrastructure that turns trading into a legitimate profession — worldwide.

VORNIX was founded by Jatin Sharma after identifying a fundamental and universal gap in the global trading industry: millions of talented traders enter markets every year, yet the overwhelming majority fail — not because they lack skill or intelligence, but because the system around them is broken.

Trading is treated as gambling in most parts of the world. Yet risk exists in every profession. An engineer studies for years with no guarantee of employment. A doctor spends a decade in education. The difference is that those professions have structured pathways — and trading does not. Not yet.

VORNIX was created to change that. By combining rigorous evaluation, real capital access and a technology ecosystem, we are building the infrastructure that makes trading a legitimate, structured, scalable profession — for anyone, anywhere in the world.

"Trading is one of the fastest-growing financial activities on earth. Yet most participants fail — not because they lack talent, but because no structured system exists to develop them."

— JATIN SHARMA, FOUNDER · VORNIX

// FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

Which evaluation program should I choose?+
Speed (manual only): 1-Step. Balance + news trading (manual only): 2-Step. EA/Algo allowed + maximum structure: 3-Step. Cost concern with EA/Algo: Partial Payment at 35% upfront.
Are EA and algorithmic bots allowed?+
EA/Algo bots are not permitted on the 1-Step or 2-Step programs. They are fully permitted on the 3-Step and Partial Payment programs in all phases and the funded account.
What is the 15% consistency rule?+
On 3-Step and Partial programs, no single profitable trade may contribute more than 15% of total accumulated profit. If total profit = $1,000, no single trade can have made more than $150. Active in all phases and the funded account.
What is the news trading rule on 3-Step and Partial?+
No trades may be open within 5 minutes before or after a high-impact news event. If the trade closes in profit — the profit is disqualified. If in loss — the full loss is counted. Applies in all phases and the funded account.
How does the trailing daily loss work?+
The daily loss limit is calculated from your highest equity point during that trading day. It trails upward as equity grows but never moves downward. It resets at the start of each new trading day.
How does the Partial Payment program work?+
Pay 35% of the evaluation fee upfront. Complete all 3 evaluation phases. Once funded and your first profit withdrawal is processed, the remaining 65% of the fee is automatically deducted from that withdrawal. No additional payment ever comes out of pocket.
When can I withdraw profits from a funded account?+
Profit withdrawals are available as soon as you generate profit on your funded account. No arbitrary waiting periods. Processed via cryptocurrency, Wise and bank transfer within 1–3 business days.
When do AI tools and the Academy launch?+
The evaluation and funding platform is live now. The Academy and all technology products are in active development. Join the waitlist via the contact form above to be among the first to access them when they launch.
