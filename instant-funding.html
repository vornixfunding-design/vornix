💳 35% Upfront — How It Works

Pay 35% upfront. Complete all three evaluation phases successfully. Once funded and your first profit withdrawal is processed, the remaining 65% is automatically deducted from that withdrawal. There is no additional payment required ever — it comes entirely from profits you have already earned.

📊 15% Consistency Rule

No single profitable trade may exceed 15% of total accumulated profit. Example: total profit $1,000 → no single trade may have contributed more than $150. This applies in all phases and the funded account.

📰 No News Trading

Any trade opened within 5 minutes before or after a high-impact news event: if in profit — profit is disqualified. If in loss — loss is fully counted. Plan your trades around economic calendar events.
