DRAWDOWN & LOSS RULES

Loss limits are the most critical rules in any evaluation. Every VORNIX program uses two simultaneous drawdown mechanisms — a trailing daily loss limit and a static maximum drawdown. Both must be respected at all times. Breaching either one terminates the evaluation immediately with no exceptions.

Daily Loss Limit — Trailing
How It Is Calculated
The daily loss limit is calculated from the highest equity point reached during that trading day, not from the opening balance. The floor rises as your equity rises — it only ever moves upward, never downward. It resets at 00:00 server time each day.
Example (1-Step, 3% daily loss, $10,000 account): Equity peaks at $10,800 during the day. Your floor becomes $10,800 − 3% = $10,476. If equity drops below $10,476 at any moment, the evaluation terminates immediately, even if your balance is still above your starting value.
Maximum Drawdown — Static
How It Is Calculated
The maximum drawdown is calculated from the initial account balance only. It is a fixed, permanent floor that never changes regardless of how much profit you generate. It applies at all times, including during open trades. Your account equity (not just balance) must stay above this floor continuously.
Example (1-Step, 8% max drawdown, $10,000 account): Max drawdown floor = $10,000 − 8% = $9,200. Even with a $12,000 balance, your equity cannot fall below $9,200 at any point.

PROFIT TARGETS BY PROGRAM

Each evaluation program requires you to reach a specified profit target within the defined phases. Profit is calculated as a percentage of the initial account balance. Only fully closed trades count — floating (unrealised) profit does not contribute to the target. The target must be met while simultaneously respecting all drawdown and other applicable rules.

⚡ 1-Step Program
Single Phase — 10% Target
Evaluation Phase: 10% profit on initial balance.
Funded Account: No profit target — trade and withdraw freely.

No minimum trading days. No time limit. News trading fully permitted. Manual trading only — no EA or algorithmic bots.
🎯 2-Step Program
Two Phases — 10% + 5%
Phase 1: 10% profit on initial balance.
Phase 2: 5% profit on initial balance.
Funded Account: No profit target — full trading freedom.

Minimum 3 trading days per phase. News trading permitted in both phases. Manual trading only.
🏆 3-Step Program
Three Phases — 8% + 5% + 4%
Phase 1: 8% profit on initial balance.
Phase 2: 5% profit on initial balance.
Phase 3: 4% profit on initial balance.
Funded Account: No profit target — trade freely.

Minimum 3 trading days per phase. No news trading permitted. EA/Algo bots allowed throughout.
💳 Partial Payment Program
Three Phases — 10% + 6% + 5%
Phase 1: 10% profit on initial balance.
Phase 2: 6% profit on initial balance.
Phase 3: 5% profit on initial balance.
Funded Account: No profit target.

Minimum 5 trading days per phase. No news trading. 15% consistency rule applies. EA/Algo permitted.
How Profit Is Measured
Profit is measured in monetary terms against the initial balance. On a $10,000 account with a 10% target, you need $1,000 in closed profit. Only fully closed positions count. Open floating P&L, swap credits, and unrealised gains do not count toward the target under any program.

CONSISTENCY RULES

The consistency rule ensures evaluation profit is generated through repeated, structured trading rather than a single large trade. It limits the contribution any single trade can make to your total profit. On stricter programs, it also applies to the funded account itself.

40% Cap — 1-Step & 2-Step
No Single Trade Exceeds 40% of Total Profit
On the 1-Step and 2-Step programs, no individual closed trade may contribute more than 40% of your total accumulated profit at the point of evaluation pass review.
Example: Total profit is $1,000. No single trade may have contributed more than $400. A single $450 trade violates the consistency rule even if all other trades were within limits.
15% Cap — 3-Step & Partial
No Single Trade Exceeds 15% of Total Profit
On the 3-Step and Partial programs, the cap is significantly stricter. No individual closed trade may contribute more than 15% of total accumulated profit. This applies across all evaluation phases and on the funded account itself.
Example: Total profit is $2,000. No single trade may have contributed more than $300. A $350 trade violates the rule regardless of all other trades being within limits.

MINIMUM TRADING DAYS

Some programs require a minimum number of trading days before an evaluation phase can pass. A trading day is any calendar day on which at least one trade is both opened and fully closed. Holding positions overnight does not create additional trading days when the calendar changes.

⚡ 1-Step Program
No Minimum Trading Days
There is no minimum trading day requirement on the 1-Step program. You may pass the evaluation in as few as one trading day, provided the profit target is met and all drawdown rules are respected throughout.
🎯 2-Step Program
3 Minimum Trading Days Per Phase
Both Phase 1 and Phase 2 each require a minimum of 3 trading days before the phase can pass. You may reach the profit target before 3 days, but the phase will not be approved until 3 qualifying trading days have been recorded independently for that phase.
🏆 3-Step Program
3 Minimum Trading Days Per Phase
All three phases each require a minimum of 3 trading days independently. Each phase must satisfy its own 3-day minimum before progression to the next phase or to the funded account is permitted.
💳 Partial Payment Program
5 Minimum Trading Days Per Phase
All three phases each require a minimum of 5 trading days. This is the strictest minimum day requirement across all VORNIX programs. Each phase must independently satisfy 5 qualifying trading days before progression is permitted.
What Counts as a Trading Day
A trading day requires at least one fully completed round-trip trade opened and closed on the same calendar day. Multiple trades in a single day count as one trading day. Weekend days cannot qualify. Holding a position overnight does not create an additional trading day when the calendar changes.

EA & ALGORITHMIC TRADING

Expert Advisors (EAs) and algorithmic trading are permitted on some VORNIX programs and strictly prohibited on others. The rules below define clearly which programs allow automation and which require exclusively manual trading.

✗ 1-Step — EA/Algo NOT Permitted
Manual Trading Only
All trade entries and exits must be placed manually by the account holder. Expert Advisors, automated bots, algorithmic execution scripts, and copy-trading services are strictly prohibited on all 1-Step accounts. Violations result in immediate evaluation termination without refund.
✗ 2-Step — EA/Algo NOT Permitted
Manual Trading Only
The 2-Step program also requires fully manual trading in both evaluation phases and on the funded account. EA bots, automated scripts, and copy trading from external signals are all prohibited. All execution must be performed manually by the registered account holder.
✓ 3-Step — EA/Algo Permitted
Automated Trading Allowed
EA and algorithmic trading is fully permitted on the 3-Step program across all evaluation phases and the funded account, provided all other trading rules are respected. Custom-built EAs, commercially available bots, and VPS-hosted automated systems are permitted. Tick scalping and latency arbitrage remain prohibited universally.
✓ Partial — EA/Algo Permitted
Automated Trading Allowed
EA and algorithmic trading is fully permitted on the Partial Payment program across all evaluation phases and the funded account. The 15% consistency rule and news trading restriction remain active regardless of whether trading is automated or manual. EA users must ensure their algorithms comply with all applicable rules.
✗ Universally Prohibited — All Programs
High-Frequency & Exploitative Strategies
The following algorithmic practices are prohibited on all programs regardless of whether EA trading is generally permitted on that program:

Tick Scalping / HFT: Any strategy whose primary execution model involves holding trades open for fewer than 2 minutes is not permitted.

Latency Arbitrage: Any strategy exploiting price feed delays, platform latency differences, or data connection discrepancies between brokers is strictly prohibited. Trades identified as latency arbitrage will be reversed and the account permanently terminated without refund.

Platform Exploitation: Any automated tool designed to exploit platform pricing errors, off-market quotes, or stale prices is prohibited across all accounts and programs.

NEWS TRADING RULES

News trading rules vary by program. Two programs permit news trading freely. Two programs apply asymmetric restrictions around high-impact economic events. The rules below define precisely which programs restrict news trading and the exact consequences for trades placed within restricted windows.

✓ 1-Step — News Trading Fully Permitted
No News Restrictions
The 1-Step program has no restrictions on news trading. You may open, hold, and close positions at any time, including immediately before, during, or after high-impact economic data releases. All trades are counted equally for the profit target regardless of when they are placed relative to news events.
✓ 2-Step — News Trading Fully Permitted
No News Restrictions
The 2-Step program has no restrictions on news trading in either evaluation phase or on the funded account. High-impact news event trades are treated identically to all other trades. Profits count in full and losses count in full, with no special handling.
✗ 3-Step — News Trading Restricted
5-Minute Window Rule — All Phases & Funded
Trading within a 5-minute window before or after any high-impact news event triggers asymmetric treatment on the 3-Step program:

• Trade in profit at review: profit is fully disqualified and removed from your total.
• Trade in loss at review: loss is fully counted against your drawdown limits.

This rule applies to every evaluation phase and remains active on the funded account.
High-impact events include: NFP, CPI, FOMC decisions, interest rate announcements, GDP releases, and other Tier-1 economic data. Use an economic calendar to identify restricted windows before placing any trade.
✗ Partial — News Trading Restricted
5-Minute Window Rule — All Phases & Funded
The Partial program applies the same asymmetric news restriction as the 3-Step program. Trading within 5 minutes before or after a high-impact news event:

• Trade in profit: profit is fully disqualified.
• Trade in loss: loss is fully counted.

This rule is active across all three evaluation phases and on the funded account. EA users on the Partial program must ensure their algorithms do not execute trades during news windows.

PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES

The following activities are strictly prohibited across all VORNIX programs and accounts. Violation of any rule in this section results in immediate and permanent account termination with no warnings, no appeals, and no refund. These rules apply to all evaluations, funded accounts, and any future accounts associated with the same trader.

✗ Account Sharing
Single User — No Third-Party Access
Only the individual whose name, identification, and payment are registered may trade the account. The following are all prohibited arrangements:

• Allowing another person to log in and trade on your behalf
• Receiving automated signals from a third-party service executing trades on your account
• Granting API access to an external party or service
• Any arrangement where a third party controls, influences, or executes trades on your account in any manner
✗ Cross-Account Hedging
No Opposing Positions Across VORNIX Accounts
Hedging between two or more VORNIX accounts simultaneously is prohibited. You may not hold a long position on one VORNIX account and a short position on a different VORNIX account for the same instrument at the same time. This applies even if both accounts are registered under the same name.

Normal hedging within a single account as part of a legitimate trading strategy is permitted, subject to all other applicable rules.
✗ Exploitative Strategies
Arbitrage, Manipulation & Platform Exploitation
Any strategy profiting from broker pricing errors, platform connectivity issues, data feed delays, or any platform anomaly rather than genuine market analysis is prohibited. This includes:

• Latency arbitrage and price feed exploitation
• Tick scalping as a primary strategy (trades regularly held under 2 minutes)
• Grid strategies designed to exploit platform pricing mechanics
• Martingale systems with excessive, unmanaged position accumulation
• Any strategy structured to systematically abuse the evaluation system
✗ Identity Fraud
Accurate Identity Required on All Accounts
All registrations must use accurate personal information matching the individual making the purchase. The following are all prohibited:

• Registering under a false name or identity
• Using another individual's payment method without their explicit authorisation
• Submitting false or altered documentation during KYC verification
• Creating multiple accounts to bypass risk management rules, evaluation limits, or per-person restrictions

Accounts associated with fraudulent registration will be permanently terminated and referred for further action.

SCALING PROGRAM

All VORNIX funded accounts include an automatic scaling program. As you demonstrate consistent profitability on your funded account, your capital allocation increases automatically — without any separate application, approval process, or additional fee required.

Automatic 25% Allocation Increase
How the Scaling Program Works
Every time you generate 10% cumulative profit on your funded account, your account allocation automatically increases by 25%.

Scaling example: You begin with a $20,000 funded account. After generating 10% profit ($2,000 in closed profit), your allocation increases to $25,000. After the next 10% profit cycle on the $25,000 account, your allocation increases further to $31,250 — and so on.

Maximum allocation: $100,000. No funded account under the standard program exceeds this limit. Your allocation will stop scaling once you reach $100,000.

Automatic scaling applies to all four evaluation programs. There is no additional fee, no separate application, and no processing delay — the new allocation is applied automatically upon reaching the 10% cumulative profit milestone.

ACCOUNT RESETS

If your evaluation account is terminated due to a rule breach, you may purchase a reset at a discounted fee to continue your attempt. A reset reinstates your account at the same phase where the breach occurred, with the same initial balance and all original program parameters.

Reset Policy — Complete Details
Restart Without Paying the Full Fee Again
What a reset includes:
• Account reinstated at the specific phase where the rule breach occurred.
• Initial balance restored to the original evaluation starting balance for that account size.
• All prior trading history from the terminated attempt is cleared completely.
• The same program rules that applied on the original purchase continue to apply on the reset.

What a reset does not include:
• Any profit generated during the previous attempt is not carried over to the reset account.
• Resets cannot be applied to funded accounts — funded accounts that are terminated cannot be reset under any circumstances.
• A reset does not change the program type, account size, or any rule parameters.

Pricing: Reset fees are discounted relative to the original evaluation fee and vary by program and account size. Contact the VORNIX support team for current reset pricing on your specific account.

KYC & VERIFICATION

Know Your Customer (KYC) identity verification is required before your first funded account profit withdrawal. The process is completed once and applies permanently to your account. Once verified, all future withdrawals proceed without any additional verification steps.

Required Documents
What You Need to Submit
1. Government-issued photo identification:
Passport, national identity card, or government-issued driving licence. Must clearly show your full legal name, a current photograph, and the document's expiry date. Expired documents are not accepted.

2. Proof of residential address:
A utility bill (gas, electricity, water, or internet), bank statement, or government-issued letter dated within the last 90 days, clearly showing your full legal name and current residential address.

All documents must be submitted in clear digital format (photograph or scanned copy). Blurred, cropped, obscured, or digitally altered documents will not be accepted and will require resubmission.
One-Time Verification
Complete Once — Withdraw Without Restriction
KYC verification is a one-time process only. Once your identity and address have been successfully verified by the VORNIX compliance team:

• All future withdrawals on all your VORNIX accounts process without any further verification requirements.
• Verification status is permanently tied to your registered email address and identity on file.
• Additional funded accounts registered under the same verified identity do not require re-verification.

When KYC is required: Before your first profit withdrawal on a funded account only. KYC verification is not required to purchase, begin, or complete an evaluation. It only becomes required at the point of your first payout request.
