// Trading Rules

VORNIX RULES

Every rule clearly explained. No hidden conditions. Read this before starting any evaluation — know exactly what is and is not permitted.

// Section 01

CORE TRADING RULES

These rules apply to every account — evaluation and funded — unless a specific program exception is noted.

📉 Daily Loss Limit — Trailing

Calculated from the highest equity point of that trading day. If your equity reaches $10,500 on a $10,000 account with 5% daily loss — your floor becomes $9,975. Trails upward only. Resets at 00:00 server time each day.

🛑 Max Drawdown — Static

Calculated from the initial account balance only. Never adjusts regardless of profits. On a $10,000 account with 8% max drawdown, your absolute floor is always $9,200.

📊 Consistency Rule

1-Step & 2-Step: No single trade may contribute more than 40% of total profit.

3-Step & Partial: No single trade may contribute more than 15% of total profit.

📅 Minimum Trading Days

1-Step: No minimum days.
2-Step & 3-Step: Minimum 3 trading days per phase.
Partial: Minimum 5 trading days per phase.
A trading day = at least one trade opened and closed.

🕐 No Time Limit

All VORNIX evaluation programs have no time limit. Take as long as you need to pass, provided you stay within the drawdown rules. There is no expiry date.

📈 Tradeable Instruments

All programs allow: Forex (major, minor and exotic pairs), Indices (US30, NAS100, SPX500, UK100, GER40), Commodities (Gold, Silver, Oil), Crypto and Stocks.

// Section 02

EA & ALGO RULES

EA and algorithmic trading rules vary by program. Read carefully before purchasing.

✗ 1-Step — No EA/Algo

EA and algorithmic bots are not permitted on the 1-Step program. Manual trading only. Any trades executed via automated systems will be disqualified.

✗ 2-Step — No EA/Algo

EA and algorithmic bots are not permitted on the 2-Step program. Manual trading only. Any trades executed via automated systems will be disqualified.

✓ 3-Step — EA/Algo Allowed

EA and algorithmic trading is permitted on the 3-Step program in all phases and the funded account, provided all other rules are respected.

✓ Partial — EA/Algo Allowed

EA and algorithmic trading is permitted on the Partial Payment program in all phases and the funded account, provided all other rules are respected.

✗ Tick Scalping & HFT

Strategies that hold trades for less than 2 minutes as a primary method are not permitted on any program. This targets algorithmic exploitation only.

⚠ Latency Arbitrage

Any strategy exploiting platform latency, price feed delays or broker errors is prohibited across all programs. Such trades will be reversed and the account terminated.

// Section 03

PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES

These activities result in immediate account termination. There are no warnings.

🚫 News Trading — 3-Step & Partial Only

Trades within 5 minutes before or after a high-impact news event: profit is disqualified. Loss is fully counted. News trading is fully allowed on 1-Step and 2-Step.

🚫 Account Sharing

Only the registered account holder may trade. Account sharing, copy trading from external signals, or third-party access is strictly prohibited and results in immediate termination.

🚫 Opposite Account Hedging

Hedging between two VORNIX accounts simultaneously is prohibited. Hedging within a single account under a normal trading strategy is permitted.

// Section 04

PAYOUT & WITHDRAWAL RULES

💰 Fast Withdrawals

Profit withdrawals are available as soon as you generate profit on your funded account. No arbitrary waiting periods. Processed via cryptocurrency, Wise and bank transfer within 1–3 business days.

💰 Profit Split

1-Step: 95% of all profits.
2-Step: 90% of all profits.
3-Step & Partial: 80% rising to 90% after scaling milestones.

💰 Payment Methods

Payouts via: Cryptocurrency (USDT, USDC, BTC), Wise and Bank Transfer. Processing time: 1–3 business days after approval.

💰 Partial Payment Deduction

Remaining 65% of evaluation fee automatically deducted from your first profit withdrawal only. All subsequent withdrawals receive the full profit split.

💰 Minimum Withdrawal

The minimum withdrawal amount is $50. There is no maximum withdrawal limit.

💰 KYC Verification

Before your first payout: government-issued photo ID and proof of address. One-time process only. All subsequent payouts process without re-verification.

⚠ Risk Disclaimer

Trading financial instruments involves significant risk of loss and is not suitable for all investors. Past performance is not indicative of future results. VORNIX evaluation accounts use simulated capital for the purpose of skill assessment. Funded accounts trade real capital subject to the profit split arrangement. Always trade responsibly.
